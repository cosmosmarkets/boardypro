## The 30-second version

- **Boardy** is an AI superconnector. A voice-first agent (cardboard-box-head character, Australian accent, "half OpenAI, half Pixar") that calls you, learns your goals, and makes warm intros from its network of founders and investors.
- **Boardy Pro** ($100/mo) is the pivot from *connector to closer*: it owns the full deal pipeline, joins calls, follows up as itself, and compounds over time. "I'm done making intros. Now I make deals happen."
- The **launch** went viral organically: 1M+ views, 5,000 free spots gone in 2 hours, zero ad spend.
- Built by **Andrew D'Souza** (Clearco founder, $5B+ deployed). The agent reportedly raised its own $8M round on its own platform.
- **Design direction:** golden Boardy on a dark hero (gold = premium tier), warm peach floor-glow, oversized "PRO", copy written in Boardy's first-person voice.
