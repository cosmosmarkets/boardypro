# Boardy Pro Landing Page

Research, brand direction, and build specs for a landing page for **Boardy Pro**, the paid tier of [Boardy](https://boardy.ai), the AI superconnector.

The pitch in one line, in Boardy's voice: *"I'm done making intros. Now I make deals happen."* Free Boardy connects you. Pro closes the deal.

The page tells that story as **a living network you travel through.** The whole page is one node-edge graph you scroll left to right. Rightward is connector to closer.

## The locked design direction

- **One living network.** The whole page is a single connected node-edge graph, scrolled left to right. Not a stack of sections. Direction carries meaning: rightward is intro to closed deal.
- **Boardy is a quiet brass left anchor.** His edges fan rightward across every screen, so everything you meet traces back to him. He bookends: present at the entry, returns at the close when your node connects. Blue face kept intact.
- **The deal-track spine.** A bottom spine with a playhead, stops `Intro -> Proof -> Pipeline -> Differentiator -> $8M round -> Network -> Close`. It is the wayfinding. No minimap.
- **Your graph comes alive.** A dim "You" node gains a connection and more light at each cluster and ends fully lit Boardy-blue and clickable. Real logos and named investors in the outer ring, archetypal role-labels near you.
- **The peak: the $8M self-loop.** Boardy's edge loops back into himself, the proof he closed his own round on his own platform.
- **Cool, near-black stage.** Brass glows on cool dark and dies on warm or light backgrounds. Warm-white light at the bookends only.
- **Phone-first CTA.** A live phone field at the Close plus a persistent docked "Talk to Boardy". Drop your number, Boardy calls you. Free for your first year, then $100/mo.
- **Voice is Boardy.** First person, warm, a little cheeky. Never corporate. No em dashes.

Full rationale and the open-vs-locked decisions are in the specs above. Start with [06-stage-and-graph-spec](boardy-pro-research/06-stage-and-graph-spec.md).
