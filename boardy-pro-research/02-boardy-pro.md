# Boardy Pro: The Product You're Designing For

## The pivot, in one line

> "I'm done making intros. Boardy Pro is here. Now I make deals happen."

This is the core story. **Free Boardy connects. Pro closes.** Free Boardy stops at the handshake. Pro owns the entire pipeline, from first signal to next step to actual outcome.

This single before/after (connector → closer) should be the spine of the landing page.

## What Pro actually does

Pro shifts Boardy from making introductions to **running the full deal cycle:**

- **Pressure-tests fit before you spend time.** Boardy vets the opportunity so you only engage with what's real.
- **Joins calls as an active participant**, not just a notetaker. He's in the room.
- **Records notes and tracks action items** across the cycle.
- **Handles scheduling and prep** automatically.
- **Nudges stalled threads** to keep momentum, so deals don't die in someone's inbox.
- **Operates under his own identity.** Boardy sends the emails and calendar invites *as Boardy*, not ghostwriting as you.
- **Learns and compounds.** Every deal cycle teaches him more about your network, preferences, and style.

## The killer differentiator (give this its own section)

Because Boardy follows up **as himself**, persistent chasing doesn't make *you* look desperate. A founder nagging an investor reads badly. Boardy nagging on your behalf is just Boardy doing his job. This removes the social friction that kills most follow-up. It's a genuine wedge, not a feature bullet.

## Pricing

- **$100 / month.**
- The argument (per Slava Kurilyak's writeup) is that subscription beats one-time pricing for a negotiation agent because the value **compounds per deal cycle**: the agent keeps getting better at your network and your style.

## The launch story (use it as social proof)

The Pro launch is itself a conversion asset. The numbers:

- **4 posts** across X and LinkedIn.
- **1M+ views, ~20,000 comments.**
- First **5,000 signups got Pro free** (free for life / free for a year depending on source).
- Those 5,000 spots filled **in 2 hours.**
- **$0 in paid ads. No influencers.** Trended on X organically, driven entirely by community.

Lead with these. "5,000 spots gone in 2 hours, zero ad spend" does more for credibility than any feature list.

## Onboarding mechanic (current)

The live Pro entry (onboarding.boardy.ai) is dead simple: enter your phone number, verify, continue. The product is voice/phone-native, so the CTA funnels to a phone number, not a typical email signup. Worth mirroring in the page design: the conversion action is "start talking to Boardy," not "create an account."

## Positioning summary

| | Free Boardy | Boardy Pro |
|---|---|---|
| Job | Make the intro | Close the deal |
| Scope | Stops at handshake | Owns the full pipeline |
| Presence | Matches and emails | Joins calls, follows up, tracks |
| Identity | Connector | Closer / deal agent |
| Price | Free (3 intros/day) | $100/mo |
