# Boardy: Team, Funding, and Context

## Founding team

Boardy was conceived in **March 2024**, built over the summer, and launched in **October 2024**.

- **Andrew D'Souza** — Co-founder and CEO. The face and origin of the network.
- **Matt Stein** — Co-founder.
- **Shen Sivananthan** — Co-founder.
- **Ankur Boyed** — Co-founder (brother of Abhinav).
- **Abhinav Boyed** — Co-founder (brother of Ankur).

## Andrew D'Souza background (the credibility anchor)

- Co-founded **Clearco** (originally Clearbanc) in 2015 alongside **Michele Romanow**.
- Clearco became one of Canada's most prominent **fintech unicorns**: deployed **$5B+** to thousands of businesses, scaled to **~600 employees**, passed **$100M in revenue**.
- Spent nearly **10 years** at Clearco. Stepped back as CEO when the company needed a more seasoned capital-markets leader, then turned to AI and built Boardy.
- His Clearco network is literally the seed of Boardy's network. Most early Boardy investors came from his Clearco relationships.

This pedigree matters for the landing page: the network is credible because the founder's network is credible.

## Funding

- **Pre-seed: $3M** (announced October 2024). Led by **HF0**, with **8VC, Precursor, Afore, FJ Labs, and NextView** participating.
- **Later round: $8M USD**, led by **Creandum**. Notably, Boardy reportedly **raised much of this round on its own platform**, the AI agent networking its way to its own investors. Total raised reported at **$10M+**.

## Origin story / mission (for an "about" or founder section)

The team built Boardy as a deliberate counter to the loneliness amplified by social media and AI. Rather than build an AI companion that substitutes for human relationships, they built an AI that drives you toward real human connection.

Anchor quote:
> "We built Boardy to create a better future, where AI actually makes us more connected to each other." — Andrew D'Souza

## Why this is good landing-page material
