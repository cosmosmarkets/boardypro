# Boardy: What It Is and What It's About

## One-liner

Boardy is an **AI superconnector**. A voice-first AI agent that talks to you on the phone, learns what you're building and who you need, and brokers warm, double opt-in introductions from its network of founders, investors, and operators.

Current site headline: *"Hey, I'm Boardy, The AI Superconnector! I know who you should meet before you do. Let me make the intro."*

## The character (this is the whole brand)

Boardy is not a dashboard or a SaaS tool. Boardy is a **persona**:

- A guy with a **cardboard box for a head**, wearing a suit.
- Speaks with a **friendly Australian accent** that disarms people instantly.
- The founder's own framing, and the single most important brand line to internalize: **"half OpenAI, half Pixar."** Frontier AI capability wrapped in a warm, memorable, almost cartoon character.
- On LinkedIn he's "that funny guy with a cardboard box for a head, dropping comments and posting what seems like absolute nonsense under serious LinkedIn posts." Cheeky, omnipresent, hard to ignore.

The character is the moat. Everything (copy, design, CTAs) should feel like you are interacting with Boardy himself, not with a company that makes Boardy.

## How the free product works

1. **Connect** on LinkedIn (search "Boardy", send a request).
2. **DM him** a short note about what you're working on.
3. **He calls you.** Real phone call, human-sounding AI voice (WhatsApp text alternative exists).
4. **You talk naturally** about your goals and who you need to meet.
5. **He matches** against his network, judging both relevance *and* whether the two of you would actually get along.
6. **Double opt-in intro.** If both sides say yes, Boardy sends a warm intro over email with context.
7. **You debrief** him on how it went, which sharpens future matches.

Free tier: unlimited conversations, intros capped at roughly **3 per day**.

## The mission / why it exists

The team's stated reason for building Boardy is a reaction against the loneliness that social media and AI have deepened. Instead of building an AI *companion* that replaces human contact, they built an AI that **pushes you back toward real people.**

Founder quote to anchor messaging:
> "We built Boardy to create a better future, where AI actually makes us more connected to each other."

## The network

- Started as founder Andrew D'Souza's personal rolodex from his Clearco years.
- Now spans several thousand people: founders, investors, creators, operators.
- Logos surfaced on the site: a16z, Sequoia, Anthropic, OpenAI, Nvidia, Google, xAI, Tesla, HF0, AI Fund.
- Headline metrics the site tracks (live values as of last scrape, June 2026): **166,263 people spoken with, 114,627 introductions made, $63B capital introduced, 17h average time to match.** Round down for landing-page use (166K+, 110K+, $63B+). Note: these are JS-injected on boardy.ai, so a static page scrape returns placeholder zeros.

## Why people pay attention

- Boardy famously **raised its own funding round** by networking on its own platform (the $8M round led by Creandum).
- It has been written up as an AI agent "outperforming VCs" at making the right intro.
- The persona-led, organic growth motion (no ads) is itself part of the story.
