# Boardy Pro Landing Page: Cluster Flow Spec

Status: build spec for the journey. The page is one horizontal node-edge graph (system spec in [06-stage-and-graph-spec.md](06-stage-and-graph-spec.md)). This file is the cluster-by-cluster content: what you meet at each stop on the deal-track spine as you scroll left to right. Voice is Boardy, first person, no em dashes. Reuses the palette, type, and graph language from 06.

## The journey

Seven clusters, strung left to right along the deal-track spine. Rightward is connector to closer.

`Intro -> Proof -> Pipeline -> Differentiator -> $8M round -> Network -> Close`

The old "transformation" section is gone as a standalone beat: the whole left-to-right movement *is* the transformation, so it does not need its own stop. The old "mission" section is demoted to an optional one-line grace-note at the Close (still open, see CLAUDE.md).

Each cluster earns the next. We open with the pivot, prove velocity, show the mechanism, defend the one objection, land the killer proof, show the network, then ask for the number. The "You" node gains a connection and a little more light at every stop, and goes fully live at the Close.

## Cluster 1 — Intro (the entry, far left)
**Stop label:** "Intro". **Spine:** playhead at the far left.

**Purpose:** First paint. Carry the whole meaning before anyone scrolls, and establish Boardy as the origin of everything to the right.

**Graph state:** Boardy sits at the far left in a warm-white pool, brass edges already fanning rightward into the dark (all `edge-potential`, dim and dashed, hinting at everything to come). The "You" node sits at the center-edge as `node-you-dim`: grey, unlit, unconnected. One first brass edge is poised between Boardy and you.

**Copy:**
- Headline (the locked line, two lines): "I'm done making intros." / "Now I make deals happen."
- Subhead: "I pressure-test the fit, sit in on the calls, and chase the follow-ups so the deal actually closes. You take the win."
- Small wayfinding nudge (in voice, optional): "Scroll right. Watch me wire you in."

**On first scroll:** the first edge draws from Boardy to your node. Your node ticks up one step of light. The journey has begun.

## Cluster 2 — Proof
**Stop label:** "Proof". 

**Purpose:** Immediate credibility. Cash the real numbers Boardy already racked up making intros, then point forward: that was the warm-up, closing is the next act.

**Graph state:** four stat-nodes light up around Boardy's fan as the edges draw in. These are the foundation Pro is built on.

**Stat-nodes (live from boardy.ai, rounded down):**
- `166,000+` people I've spoken with
- `110,000+` introductions I've made
- `$63B+` in capital introduced
- `17h` average time to match

(Exact live values as of last scrape: 166,263 spoken with, 114,627 intros, $63B capital, 17h match. Round down for honesty and longevity. Pull live if the build can. Verify $63B before printing.)

**Kicker (in voice, the Pro pivot):** "And that was just me making the intros. Now I stick around to close them."

(Alt: "All of that was the warm-up. Pro is where I close.")

**Note:** the launch-velocity numbers (1M+ views, 20K comments, 5,000 spots in 2hrs, $0 ads) are not here. They live near the $8M round / Close as supporting social proof.

## Cluster 3 — Pipeline
**Stop label:** "Pipeline".

**Purpose:** Make "I close deals" concrete. Show the actual mechanism so it is believable, not magic.

**Graph state:** a threaded path of five step-nodes that Boardy's edge runs through in order, left to right, like a deal moving down a pipe. The "You" node rides along it. Each step-node draws in as you reach it.

**Steps (each written as Boardy speaking):**
1. **"I pressure-test the fit."** Before you spend a minute, I tell you if it's actually worth your time.
2. **"I get us in the room."** I handle the scheduling and the prep, then I show up on the call myself.
3. **"I keep us honest on the call."** I take the notes, track what was agreed, and catch what matters.
4. **"I chase the follow-up."** I nudge the stalled threads so the deal keeps moving. As myself, so you never look desperate.
5. **"I get sharper every time."** Every deal teaches me more about how you work. The next one goes smoother.

**Cluster label copy:** "Here's how I close." (alt: "What I actually do.")

## Cluster 4 — Differentiator (the dying edge)
**Stop label:** "Differentiator".

**Purpose:** Defend the one real objection ("isn't it weird or spammy?") and turn it into the single strongest feature. This is the wedge.

**Graph state (the locked dying-edge moment):** a single follow-up edge between the "You" node and a target node visibly *flatlines*: its brass desaturates, the dash widens, it sags and fades to `edge-dying`. The deal is dying in someone's inbox. Then Boardy sends a brass pulse down the edge and it snaps back to `edge-made`, alive again. He revived what was dead.

**Copy:**
- Cluster label: "The follow-up is where deals die. And where I win."
- Body: "When you chase a thread, you look desperate. When I chase it, I'm just doing my job. I follow up as me, Boardy, not as a ghostwriter behind your name. So the deal keeps its momentum and you keep your leverage. That's the difference between an intro and an outcome."

## Cluster 5 — $8M round (the self-loop, the peak)
**Stop label:** "$8M round". **Spine:** the playhead parks here. Let it breathe.

**Purpose:** The strongest, most underused proof point on the page. The agent used its own product to raise its own money. Nothing else converts like this, and no competitor can fake it.

**Graph state (the locked peak):** Boardy's edge curves out and loops back into himself, a self-loop, instead of pointing outward at another node. It draws once on arrival, then holds with a slow brass shimmer. The plain claim sits right on the loop so nobody misses what it means. Creandum's mark can sit as a single node on the loop.

**Copy:**
- Eyebrow (small, this one earns it): "Not a hypothetical."
- Cluster label: "I raised my own funding round. On myself."
- Body: "I didn't just make intros for other founders. I worked my own network, found the right investors, and closed an $8M round led by Creandum. If I can do it for me, I can do it for you."
- Stat on the loop: `$8M` raised by the agent, on the agent.
- Optional supporting line (the launch velocity lands here): "Word got out. 5,000 Pro spots gone in 2 hours, zero ad spend."

## Cluster 6 — Network (your graph fills in)
**Stop label:** "Network".

**Purpose:** Substantiate that the connections are worth $100/mo. Quality of network is quality of deals. This is the literal "your graph" payoff.

**Graph state:** the graph blooms into its fullest population around the "You" node, which is now well-connected and bright (nearly live). Two rings:
- **Outer ring (the bulk):** real recognizable logos and named investors/operators as `node-real`. The aspiration and impact. a16z, Sequoia, Anthropic, OpenAI, Nvidia, Google, plus named investors and operators making up most of the ring. Framed as "the rooms I can get you into", never as endorsements. Confirm the named list before printing.
- **Inner ring (near your node):** flattering, clearly-archetypal role-labels as `node-archetype`: "a Series A lead, one intro away", "a 3-time founder", "the operator who's done this before". The personal pull. Archetypes, never named real people.

**Copy:**
- Cluster label: "The room I get you into." (alt: "Founders, investors, and operators. The people who move things.")
- Lead metric (deal quality, not just volume): `$63B+` in capital introduced.

## Cluster 7 — Close (Boardy returns, your node goes live)
**Stop label:** "Close". **Spine:** playhead at the far right. The docked CTA and this cluster's field are the same ask.

**Purpose:** Convert. The bookend: Boardy returns on the right, the warm-white light comes back, your node finishes coming alive, and the ask is a phone number.

**Graph state (the payoff):** Boardy returns at the far right in a warm-white pool (palette callback to the entry). Your "You" node crosses fully to `node-you-live`: brass-connected to the whole network behind it, Boardy-blue rimmed, glowing. It is now clickable. Clicking it focuses the phone field. The page bookends on Boardy and light, with the whole graph strung between.

**Copy (value-led):**
- Cluster label: "One closed deal pays for a decade of me."
- Subhead: "Pro is $100 a month. Your first year is free. I'll have closed something worth a lot more than that long before the bill ever shows up."
- Phone field placeholder: "Your phone number"
- CTA button: "Talk to Boardy"
- Offer line (brass, under the field): "Free for your first year."
- Reassurance microcopy (tiny): "I'll call you. No spam, no forms."
- Founding-member line (small, secondary, one line): "Founding members locked me in free for life. That window's closed, but the free year isn't."
- Optional mission grace-note (still open, one quiet line): "I was built to make us more connected, not less. Real relationships, real outcomes." Attribution if used: "Andrew D'Souza, founder. Built by the team that deployed $5B at Clearco."

**Why this close works:** leads with value (one deal dwarfs $100/mo), the free year is the offer, the founding-member detail is light FOMO not the headline, and the ask is a phone number matching Pro's real onboarding.

## Rhythm

Seven clusters. The energy alternates so no two heavy beats sit back to back: pivot (Intro) -> fast (Proof) -> detailed (Pipeline) -> sharp (Differentiator) -> big proof and a held beat (the $8M self-loop, the peak) -> broad (Network) -> warm close (Close). The "You" node lighting up is the throughline that ties them into one motion.

## Build order suggestion

Build the **vertical fallback first** (the legible source of truth, see 06 section 8): the seven clusters as a normal top-to-bottom document. Then layer the horizontal graph journey on top. Within that, start with the two bookends (Intro entry state and the Close, where Boardy appears and conversion happens), then the $8M self-loop (the peak), then fill in Proof, Pipeline, Differentiator, Network.
