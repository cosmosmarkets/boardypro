Research, brand direction, and build specs for a landing page for **Boardy Pro**, the paid tier of [Boardy](https://boardy.ai), the AI superconnector.

The pitch in one line, in Boardy's voice: *"I'm done making intros. Now I make deals happen."* Free Boardy connects you. Pro closes the deal.

## The locked design direction

- **Brushed-brass Boardy** as the hero, blue face kept intact. Brass = the premium tier signal.
- **Cool, near-black stage.** Brass glows on cool dark and dies on warm/light backgrounds.
- **The arc:** a shallow convex horizon at the base of the hero, crisp edge with a thin brass keyline, a **warm-white spotlight** (not peach) glowing behind Boardy so he reads as center-stage.
- **Oversized "PRO"** forged in brass behind the character.
- **Inline phone field** as the hero CTA (Boardy is phone-first: drop your number, he calls you). Free for your first year, then $100/mo.
- **Voice is Boardy.** First person, warm, a little cheeky. Never corporate.

Full rationale and the open-vs-locked decisions are in the specs above. Start with [06-hero-spec](boardy-pro-research/06-hero-spec.md).
